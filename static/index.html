Multitask E-commerce Recommender System

Technologies: PyTorch, Multitask Learning, Recommender Systems, Pandas

Location: La Jolla, CA | Date: Dec. 2024

Developed a comprehensive multitask learning framework with Shared-Bottom architecture featuring task-specific output layers to jointly predict click-through rate (CTR) and conversion rate (CVR), leveraging correlated user-item interaction patterns for improved prediction accuracy.

Incorporated Clippy AdaGrad, a stabilized variant of AdaGrad with dual-threshold gradient clipping (λ_rel=0.5, λ_abs=1e-2), to address sparse feature gradients common in recommendation systems, resulting in 15-20% faster convergence and improved generalization across multiple architectures.

Evaluated on real-world AliExpress e-commerce dataset (500K training samples, 5K test samples) with 16 categorical and 6 numerical features, demonstrating consistent performance improvements: Achieved a final AUC-ROC of 0.7234/0.8156 on CTR/CVR prediction, respectively, outperforming single-task baselines.

Automated Fact-Checking with BERT

Location: La Jolla, CA | Date: Dec. 2024
